"argument exercise" challenge examples are taken from exercises made
by Jon M. Young for his Critical Thinking course at Fayetteville State University:
http://faculty.uncfsu.edu/jyoung/Sample_Arguments.htm and http://faculty.uncfsu.edu/jyoung/toppage2.htm


text: text example from the argument exercise.

detailed_label: label for each sentence (i.e. of the text split at the dots .)
defining it as either a premise or conclusion of the argument, if the text
comprises an argument.

main_label: argument or not_argument.
